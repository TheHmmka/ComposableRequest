Structure User​Defaults​Storage 

public struct UserDefaultsStorage<Item: Storable>: NonThrowingStorage

A struct defining a Storage caching Items inside UserDefaults.

Do not use this in production for caching cookies or other sensible data.

Use KeycahinStorage from ComposableRequestCyprto for safe storage.

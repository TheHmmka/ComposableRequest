Protocol Storage 

public protocol Storage

A protocol defining an instance capable of caching Storables.

The definition laverages static methods in order to "hide" their definition. You should always conform to either ThrowingStorage or NonThrowingStorage, instead.

Do not use directly. Either conform to ThrowingStorage or NonThrowingStorage.
